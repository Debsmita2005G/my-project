🌾 Farmer Crop Marketplace

A web platform that helps farmers directly list and sell their crops online, reducing dependency on middlemen and giving them better market visibility.

📌 Problem Statement

Many farmers struggle to sell their crops at fair prices due to several challenges:

Dependence on middlemen who take a large portion of the profit

Limited access to digital marketplaces

Low visibility of available crops to buyers

Lack of a simple platform to showcase their produce

Because of these issues, farmers often sell crops at lower prices than market value, reducing their income.

💡 Solution

This project provides a digital marketplace where farmers can list their crops and connect with buyers directly.

The platform allows users to:

Create an account and securely log in

Add new crop listings with details such as price, location, and description

Browse crops listed by different farmers

Edit their listings when needed

Delete listings they own

By enabling direct farmer-to-buyer interaction, the platform reduces middlemen and improves price transparency and accessibility.
